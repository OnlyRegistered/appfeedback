OnlyTalk

OnlyTalk is a Node.js-based application designed for seamless voice communication with your gaming friends, supporting 6-10 users at a time. No Advertisements: Enjoy an uninterrupted experience with absolutely no ads. Resource-Efficient: The application uses minimal computer resources, allowing you to dedicate your system's power to gaming. Reliable Connection: OnlyTalk was developed to provide a hassle-free way to chat with friends, ensuring stable connections without interruptions. For safety and privacy, OnlyTalk operates exclusively over private networks. We recommend using RadminVPN for the best experience.

FileFinderTool

FileFinderTool is a powerful Node.js-based application designed to help you declutter your hard drive by searching for files that match specific patterns within a directory and its subdirectories. With the ability to identify duplicate or related files based on partial filename patterns, this tool makes it easy to target specific file types through extension searches. Users can delete files directly from the interface and set custom directories for their searches, ensuring they uncover items long forgotten. Streamline your storage and free up valuable space on your system with FileFinderTool!

Image Index

Your Image Database Creator! Create your own image database using the image files stored on your computer, and share it online within the RadminVPN private network with family and friends. Supported image formats include: .jpg, .jpeg, .gif, .png, .webp, .bmp, .tiff, .pst, .pdf, .raw, .eps, .ai, .indd, .dwg. Formats that are not supported by your browser will be automatically downloaded. The HTML structure is organized similarly to your directory structure on your hard drive, and you have the option to hide specific directories. By renaming a directory with the extension _no order _private, those directories will be excluded from the system and will not be listed. Each time you launch Image Index, you can specify your drive, and your HTML network will be created. Any changes made to your directory structure or image files will be updated automatically upon startup. The search function is designed to yield more results the fewer characters you enter. If you decide to stop using it, you can use the CleanHardDrive option to delete the entire HTML structure you’ve created, leaving no remnants behind.

Music Index

Your Music Database Creator! Create your own music database using the audio files stored on your computer and share it online within the RadminVPN private network with family and friends. Supported audio formats include: .mp3, .mp4, .webm, .aac, .ogg, .opus, .wav, .m4a, .3gp, .aiff, .au, .amr. If your browser does not support certain formats, those files will be automatically downloaded, allowing you to play them with another player, such as VLC. If you have VLC player and VLC playlists, you can download and play them directly from Music Index as well. Additionally, if you save an image in formats like .jpg, .jpeg, .gif, .png, .webp, .bmp with the same name as your audio file, that image will be displayed alongside the audio file. The HTML structure is organized similarly to your directory structure on your hard drive, and you have the option to hide specific directories. By renaming a directory with the extension _no order _private, it will be excluded from the system and not listed. Each time you launch Music Index, you can specify your drive, and your HTML network will be created. Any changes made to your directory structure or music files will be updated automatically upon startup. The search function is designed to yield more results the fewer characters you enter. If you decide to stop using it, you can utilize the CleanHardDrive option to delete the entire HTML structure you created, leaving no remnants behind.

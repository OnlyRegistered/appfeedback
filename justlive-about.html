JustLive is a multiplayer zombie survival game focused on survival, exploration, and player-driven stories.

In a world where humanity is fighting for its last chance, every decision matters. Players must adapt, cooperate, and survive in an unpredictable environment where danger can come from anywhere.

The Vision Behind JustLive

Building a Living Survival World

JustLive is more than a traditional zombie survival game.

Our vision is to create a world where players feel that every action has meaning. A place where survival is not only about collecting resources, but about choices, teamwork, exploration, and adapting to an ever-changing environment.

We want to create a multiplayer experience where players can build their own stories and where every journey feels different.

The development of JustLive will happen step by step. We are starting with the core systems and building a strong foundation using:

Unity 6

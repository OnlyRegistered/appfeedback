Join the Development Team

We are looking for passionate people who want to become part of the JustLive development community.

Who Can Join?

You do not need to be an expert. JustLive is a development community where people can learn, share ideas, improve their skills, and work together.

Whether you are a beginner, experienced developer, artist, designer, tester, or someone with great ideas — everyone can contribute.
